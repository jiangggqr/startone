                  How StartFrame works
                  
        
                  One concept at a time, grounded in your material
                  
        
                  StartFrame turns material you already have into a short, guided learning loop.
                  
        
                    
          
                  1. Add your material.StartFrame keeps verifiable source locations.
                    2. 
          
                  2. Let AI prepare the learning focus.It identifies core concepts in the material and proposes a dependency-aware path.
                    3. 
          
                  3. Give one short starting response.This first signal helps calibrate support without making you complete a setup form.
                    4. 
          
                  4. Learn the current concept.Use its explanation, Tutor, a Quiz, free recall, or a hint when you need it.
                    5. 
          
                  5. Respond to feedback.A short repair exercise closes a specific gap, then the Agent chooses one next step from validated learning evidence.
                    6. 
        
                  
        
                  If your material has a real gap: StartFrame asks before using any external source. Your uploaded material remains the primary source.
